Introduction

Joshua Hong (b. 2007) is a Korean-Mexican collegiate golfer competing in NCAA Division I for the University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA).

He entered the UTSA men’s golf program in Fall 2025. Training and tournament participation are conducted within the UTSA program under Head Coach Matt Wernecke.

Before collegiate competition, he competed in junior tournaments organized by the Mexican Golf Federation (FMG) and events recognized by the World Amateur Golf Ranking (WAGR).

Current Season Context

Having completed his first fall season (4 events) and now 4 events into the spring schedule, he has established a recent sequence of T3–1–3 over his last three completed starts, including his first collegiate individual win.

As of March 25, 2026, he is ranked No. 311 in Clippd (NCAA DI) and No. 416 in WAGR.

He followed with a top-10 finish at the Bell Bank “Pay It Forward” Collegiate (Mar 23–24, 2026), posting rounds of 73–68–71 (−4) at Wigwam Resort — Litchfield Park, Arizona, in another NCAA Division I field.

The next event on the schedule is the Tiger Invitational (Apr 6–7, 2026), to be played at The Club At Old Hawthorne — Columbia, MO, in the final regular-season event before the conference championship.
