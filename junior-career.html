Competitive Background

Between 2019 and 2025, Joshua competed in tournaments organized by the Mexican Golf Federation (FMG), the Mexico Junior Golf Tour (MJGA), and events valid for the World Amateur Golf Ranking (WAGR). He represented Mexico in international junior competitions and participated in open tournaments in South America and the United States. His junior career is documented through official results published by recognized federations and circuits.

His junior development also took place within the FMG regional system, including Zona GADO and Centro, where he benefited from structured competition, team selection, and sustained developmental support. This archive acknowledges, with appreciation, the role of those FMG structures in his progression and the opportunities made available to him through zonal and national representation in Mexico.
